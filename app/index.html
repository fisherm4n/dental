About us

We are proud of being able to offer treatment for all the family under the same roof. We have a team of experts ready to address all your dental needs. The practice offers state-of-the-art beautiful premises with comfortable cushioned dental chairs, the latest equipment and low exposure digital X-rays.

Our Multidisciplinary team of Family, Cosmetic and Invisalign® Dentists, Periodontist, Oral Surgeon, Treatment Coordinator, Dental Therapist & Hygienist, and a team of highly qualified Dental Nurses will ensure that you are provided with the best treatment tailored just for you.

Services

What we offer

We provide a wide range of dental treatments for our patients

General & Routine Dentistry

Everyday dentistry to look after your teeth and gums, from checkups to fillings and a simple scale & polish. We recommend seeing us every six months and offer affordable monthly payment plans

Smile Makeovers & Cosmetic Dentistry

From a complete smile makeover to a few small tweaks with bonding and teeth whitening our smile cosmetic dentists can give you the perfect smile, designed just for you

Dental Implants For Missing Teeth

Our Dental Implant experts offer everything from replacing a single missing tooth to more advanced implant dentistry where we can replace failing teeth or loose dentures with fixed teeth in a single day

Teeth Straightening & Braces

A wide range of braces are offered, including almost invisible clear aligners from Invisalign to put an end to those crooked teeth and unwanted gaps. We often finish treatments with teeth whitening

Emergency Dentistry

Get seen straight away by our dentists if you are in pain. It may be an abscess, broken tooth root canal or wisdom tooth that needs pulling out. We aim to get you pain-free as quickly as possible.

Crowns, Bridges & Repairs

Broken or damaged teeth? We can help offering the highest quality crowns and bridges made by our master technicians that look and feel just like the real thing.
